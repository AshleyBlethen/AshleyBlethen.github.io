1. What is a Screenshot#

Screenshots or screen captures are pictures of what is currently on your device’s display. This consists of any windows or programs that are open as well as the desktop background. Taking a screenshot on your Acer laptop can be a useful tool, whether you are capturing an online conversation or simply want to share what you are viewing on your laptop with a friend. Here’s a quick, easy guide to take screenshots on your Acer laptop.

First, open the image or program you want to capture. On your keyboard, press the Fn and Print Screen buttons at the same time. This will copy the image that’s currently on display onto your computer’s clipboard. Then, open a photo editing program such as Paint and paste the image. You can then make any edits you want to the image and save it for sharing or viewing later.

If you would rather add annotations to a screenshot, use the Snipping Tool found on Windows operating systems. To do this, open the Snipping Tool found under your programs list. Once open, click on New. You can now select the image you want to capture. You can also save the image directly from the Snipping Tool.

For Mac Operating systems, you can take screenshots directly from your keyboard. To take a full image of your screen, press the Command, Shift, and 3 keys at the same time. This will save the image on your desktop as a PNG file. If you would like to take just a section of the screen, press the Command, Shift and 4 keys at the same time. Then, drag the crosshairs until you have circled the image you want to capture.

Taking screenshots on your Acer laptop is as easy as pressing a few keys. Whether you want to take an entire image or just a portion of one, this guide shows you how.
