1. Prepare the Devices#

Before transferring your photos, it is recommended that you check your devices’ settings first. Make sure that your iPhone and Android device are discoverable and Bluetooth-enabled. If not, check the instruction manual that came with your device to activate these settings. Additionally, you will want to make sure that your devices are charged, as the file transfer will be faster. Once all settings are complete, you are now ready to start transferring your photos.

2. Steps to Transfer Pictures.#

Before proceeding to transfer the pictures from iPhone to Android, make sure that you have prepared two devices: the iPhone and the Android. To prepare them, make sure that both devices have the Bluetooth function activated. Additionally, ensure that both devices are unlocked and that the Bluetooth of the two devices can see each other.

Second, turn on the Bluetooth on both devices, and pair them by searching for the other device. This can be done by going to the Bluetooth settings on each device. After making sure the two devices can see each other, they will be paired.

Third, on the iPhone, open the folder that contains the pictures that you want to transfer. Tap the icon on the selected pictures that you want to send, then tap the “Share” option. Then, select the Bluetooth icon, and select the Android device that you want to transfer to.

Last, wait for a confirmation notification on the Android device. If it is received, then the pictures will be successfully transferred. Review the pictures to make sure the transfer was successful.
