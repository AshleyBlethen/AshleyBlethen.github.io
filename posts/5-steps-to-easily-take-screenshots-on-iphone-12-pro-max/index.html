Q1. What is the easiest way to take a screenshot on the iPhone 12 Pro Max?

A1. To take a screenshot on the iPhone 12 Pro Max, simply press the Side button and Volume Up button simultaneously and release. Your device will then automatically save the image to your Photos App.

Q2. How do I capture only a part of my screen?

A2. To capture part of the screen, press the Side button and Volume Up button simultaneously, then drag the corners of the box that appears until the desired area is captured. When you’re done, release and the image will be saved to your Photos App.

Q3. Can I save screenshots to a different folder?

A3. Yes, you can save screenshots to a different folder. To do this, open the Photos App after taking the screenshot and tap the “Edit” button. Then, select the screenshot you want to move and tap the “Share” icon in the bottom left corner. Select “Save to Files” and pick the folder where you want to save the image.

Q4. Are screenshots saved as JPEG or PNG?

A4. On the iPhone 12 Pro Max, screenshots are saved as PNG files.

Q5. Can I take a screenshot from within the Photos App?

A5. Yes, you can take a screenshot from the Photos App. To do this, open the Photos App and find the image you want to capture. Then tap the “Share” button, which looks like a rectangle with an arrow pointing out of it, and select “Take Screenshot”. The screenshot will be saved to your Photos App.
