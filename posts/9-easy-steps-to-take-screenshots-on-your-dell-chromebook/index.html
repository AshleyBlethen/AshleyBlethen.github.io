  1. Press ‘Ctrl + Switch Window’ to take a full-screen screenshot. This will capture the whole screen which you can then save or share with friends right away.

  2. To capture just a portion of the screen, press ‘Ctrl + Shift + Switch Window.’ This will allow you to select the specific area you’d like to capture.

  3. If you’d like to take a screenshot of a specific window, press ‘Ctrl + Alt + Switch Window.’ This will take a screenshot of the active window you have open.

  4. If you want to capture a full webpage, press ‘Ctrl + Shift + F5.’ This will take a snapshot of the entire webpage including any additional content that may not be visible on the screen.

  5. To take a screenshot of the login screen, press ‘Ctrl + Shift + Power.’ This will capture the entire login screen, including any passwords you have entered.

  6. If you’re having trouble taking a screenshot, you can also use the built-in ‘Screenshot’ app. Just open the app, select the type of screenshot you want to take, and hit the ‘Take Screenshot’ button.

  7. You can also use the ‘Chrome OS’ app to take screenshots. Just open the app and select the ‘Take Screenshot’ icon.

  8. Once you’ve taken your screenshot, you can save it to your Chromebook’s hard drive. Just open the ‘Files’ app, select the screenshot, and hit ‘Save.’

  9. You can also share your screenshot with others. Just press the ‘Share’ button, select who you want to share it with, and hit ‘Send.’
