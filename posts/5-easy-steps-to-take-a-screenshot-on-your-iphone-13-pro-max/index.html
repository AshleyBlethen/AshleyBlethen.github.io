Q2. How do I take a screenshot on my iPhone 13 Pro Max? A2. Q4. How do I delete screenshots? A4. You can easily delete any screenshot by opening the Photos app and tapping on the screenshot you would like to delete. Then tap the trash can icon icon located in the lower-right corner of the photo and click “Delete.”

Q5. Are screenshots saved automatically? A5. Yes, screenshots are usually saved automatically, typically in the Photos app. However, you can go to your iPhone 13 Pro Max’s settings to adjust your screenshot settings. In the Settings app, select “Photos” and then adjust the “Autosave” option to customize when and how your screenshots are saved.
