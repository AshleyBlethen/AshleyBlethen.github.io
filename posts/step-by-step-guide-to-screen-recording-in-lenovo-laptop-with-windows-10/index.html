1. Overview of Windows 10’s Screen Recorder#

Windows 10 has a screen recorder feature that allows users to record their computer activity on a Lenovo laptop. Recording can be done for educational purposes, to demo software, to record a video, and more. This tutorial will help you get started with screen recording in Windows 10 on your Lenovo laptop.

First, you need to open the Action Center by clicking on the Action Center icon in the system tray. Scroll down and locate the Screen Record option. You can also access it by pressing the Windows + G key combination. After that, you will be presented with the Game Bar that allows you to start a recording.

Next, you can select the recording length. You can choose the entire screen or part of the screen. You will also be able to adjust the settings such as microphone and webcam selection. Once you are done, you can click the Record button and start the recording process. You can also use a timer to stop the recording process automatically.

When the recording is finished, you can save the video file on your laptop or a USB drive. You can also share the recording with others by using the Windows Share feature. Once you finish the recording process, you can also edit the video with Windows Movie Maker or other video editing software.

1. Enabling the Screen Recorder#

Windows 10 has a built-in screen recorder that provides you with the ability to record your computer screen with audio. To use this feature in your Lenovo laptop, follow these simple steps. First, open the settings menu from the start menu. Here, you’ll find a ‘Game bar’ option. Click on it to enable game bar for recording. Next, press the ‘Win + G’ keys to open the Game bar. You’ll see a ‘Record’ button on the bottom. Click on it to start recording the screen. To stop the recording, open the Game bar again and click the ‘Stop’ button. Lastly, the recording will be saved in the ‘Videos’ folder on your computer. That’s it! You’ve successfully recorded your computer’s screen using Windows 10’s built-in screen recorder.

2. Recording Your Screen#

Windows 10 has recently gained the ability to capture screenshots and record videos of your screen with the built-in Snipping Tool. To use this feature, you will need to open the tool and select the type of screenshot or video you want to capture. Here are the steps to use Windows 10’s Screen Recorder on your Lenovo laptop:

  1. Go to Settings > System > Captures. You will find a window with different options for capturing your screen.

  2. Select Screenshots and then choose the type of capture you want to make – like a regular capture, a delayed capture, or a region capture.

  3. Once you select the type of capture, you can set your preferences for the capture such as resolution, output format, and file size.

  4. When you’re done with the settings, hit record. You can find the resulting file saved in the same folder where you choose to save it. That’s it! You’ve successfully used Windows 10’s Screen Recorder on your Lenovo laptop.
