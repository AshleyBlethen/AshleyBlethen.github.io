Q1 : How do I activate screen recording on my Samsung A12?

A1 : To activate screen recording on your Samsung A12, open the Quick Panel option in the notification shade and select the Screen Recorder icon. Make any necessary adjustments to the settings, then tap the Start button to begin recording.

Q2 : How do I stop the screen recording on my Samsung A12?

A2 : To stop the recording, you can either tap the Stop button on the recording notification or drag down the notification shade to open the Quick Panel and tap the Stop button there.

Q3 : How do I access the screen recording I have made on my Samsung A12?

A3 : You can access the screen recordings you have made on your Samsung A12 by launching the Gallery app and tapping on the Videos tab. You can also access the recordings by opening the Quick Panel option in the notification shade and tapping the Screen Recorder icon.

Q4 : Can I share the screen recordings I have made with other people?

A4 : Yes, you can easily share the screen recordings you have made with others via email, messaging apps or by uploading them to your favorite social media. To do so, open the Quick Panel option in the notification shade and select the Screen Recorder icon. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen and select your desired sharing option.

Q5 : Can I record the audio while I am recording the screen on my Samsung A12?

A5 : Yes, you can record the audio while recording the screen on your Samsung A12. To do so, open the Quick Panel option in the notification shade and select the Screen Recorder icon. Make sure the switch next to Audio is turned on, then tap the Start button to begin the recording.
