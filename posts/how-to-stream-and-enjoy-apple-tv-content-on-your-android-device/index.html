You can browse through the different categories of movies and shows on Apple TV’s library. To watch a movie or a show, select a title and click the Play button.

Secondly, you need to enable AirPlay streaming to watch Apple TV content on your Android device. Open the Settings menu on your device and tap the AirPlay option. You can then select the device you want to stream content from. To stream content from Apple TV on Android, choose Apple TV from the “Devices” list and hit the “Connect” button to start streaming.

Thirdly, stream any type of content from Apple TV on your Android device quickly. To do this, simply select the content option available in the Apple TV app and select the “Send to TV” option. You will get an Apple TV code that you can enter in the AirPlay option to start streaming a specific show or movie from Apple TV.

Finally, once you are finished streaming, you can disconnect the AirPlay connection by going to the device list and pressing the “Disconnect” option. After disconnecting, you can select a new pre-recorded show or movie to watch on your Android device.
