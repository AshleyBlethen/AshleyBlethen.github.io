- Troubleshooting Tips#

Mirroring your device’s screen to your Hisense Android TV is a great way to maximize the usefulness of your TV. However, if something goes wrong during the process, you may run into some troubleshooting issues. Here are some top tips for trouble-shooting screen mirroring on Hisense Android TV:

  1. Check your device’s system settings. Make sure that the device you wish to mirror from supports screen mirroring and that it has the feature enabled.
  2. Make sure you have an active and stable Wi-Fi connection. If your connection isn’t strong enough, your device may not be able to connect to the Hisense Android TV, or the process may be slow and unreliable.
  3. Check your TV’s mirroring settings. Make sure the option for screen mirroring is set to on and that you are on the same wireless network as the device you wish to mirror.
  4. Restart both the device you wish to mirror and the TV. This may help to reset any network connections that have become unstable, allowing the mirroring process to work smoothly.
