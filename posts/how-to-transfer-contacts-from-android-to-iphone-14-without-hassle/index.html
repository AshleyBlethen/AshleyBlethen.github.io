2. Transferring Contacts#

Transferring contacts from an Android phone to an iPhone 14 can be tricky. To begin, open the Contacts app on your Android phone. Once the app is opened, tap on the three vertical dots in the top right corner of the app. This will reveal a list of options, one of which being to Export the contacts. Select this option, and this will begin the exporting process.

Next, connect your Android phone to a computer via USB cable. Transfer the contacts to the computer so it can be accessed by the iPhone. Once the contacts have been transferred, disconnect the Android phone from the computer.

Next, connect the iPhone 14 to the same computer. Open iTunes and select the Contacts folder. Click the Import button and select the contacts from the Android phone. This will start the transfer process.

Once the contacts have been transferred, click the Sync button in iTunes. This will ensure the contacts are transferred to the iPhone. Finally, disconnect the iPhone from the computer and the contacts will now be on the device.
