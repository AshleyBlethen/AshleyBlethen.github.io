2. Capturing Screenshots on iPad Air 2#

Taking a screenshot on an iPad Air 2 is simple and easy. This will capture a screenshot and the image will appear momentarily in the bottom left corner of your device. You can tap the image to preview it, or pinch the image to edit and share it. If you have an Apple Pencil, you can also take a screenshot by tapping the top of your Apple Pencil twice on the display. Doing this will capture your screenshot and it will appear momentarily in the bottom left corner of your device.

You can also use Assistive Touch to take a screenshot. To enable Assistive Touch, first go to Settings > Accessibility > Physical & Motor > AssistiveTouch. Turn the switch on and a small circle will appear on your display. Tap the circle and select Device > More > Screenshot. This will capture your screenshot and it will appear momentarily in the bottom left corner of your device.
