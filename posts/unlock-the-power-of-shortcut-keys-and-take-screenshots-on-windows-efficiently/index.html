Finally, Windows 10 users are now able to take screenshots using the Snipping Tool. Open the tool before pressing the ‘Ctrl+ PrtScr’ shortcut to take a screenshot of the full screen. Alternatively, use ‘Ctrl+Shift+PrtScr’ to select a specific area, or ‘Ctrl+Alt+PrtScr’ to select a specific window.
