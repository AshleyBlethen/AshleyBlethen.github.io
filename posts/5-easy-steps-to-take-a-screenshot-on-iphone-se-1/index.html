Q.1 How do I take a screenshot on an iPhone SE? A. To take a screenshot on an iPhone SE, simply press and hold the Side button and the Volume Up button at the same time. Once you hear a camera shutter sound or feel a slight vibration, you have successfully taken a screenshot.

Q.2 What happens after I have taken a screenshot? A. After taking a screenshot, the image will be stored in your Photos app on your iPhone. You can find it in the ‘Screenshots’ folder. If you have iCloud Photos enabled, the screenshot will also be stored in the Photo Stream view as well.

Q.3 Can I edit my screenshots? A. Yes, you can. When you open the screenshot in the Photos app, you will be presented with the edit menu. You can use this to crop, rotate, enhance and retouch your screenshot.

Q.4 Can I share my screenshots? A. Yes, you can share your screenshots with anyone as an image file either via email, messaging, or any other compatible app. You can also post them directly to social media platforms such as Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, etc.

Q.5 Is there a way to mark up my screenshots? A. Yes, you can. When you open the screenshot in the Photos app, there is a ‘Markup’ option available.
