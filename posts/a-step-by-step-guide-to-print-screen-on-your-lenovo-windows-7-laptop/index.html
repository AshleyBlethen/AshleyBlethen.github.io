Print screen is a basic yet important function for any laptop user. For Lenovo Windows 7 laptop users, it’s easy to use the Print Screen feature. This step-by-step guide will walk you through the basics of taking screenshots on your Lenovo Windows 7 laptop.

To take a screenshot, simply press the “PrtSc” (Print Screen) button on the top row of your laptop keyboard. You should then see a thumbnail of the screenshot in the lower right corner of your screen. To save the screenshot to your device, press the “Window + PrtSc” keys at the same time. This will automatically save the screenshot to your device’s Pictures folder, so you can access it later.

To capture an active window, press the “Alt + PrtSc” keys at the same time. This will take a screenshot of the active window. You can then paste the screenshot to any document, image editor, or even onto your desktop. To paste the screenshot, simply press the “Ctrl + V” keys.

Finally, if you want to capture the entire screen, press the “Fn + PrtSc” keys. This will take a screenshot of the entire screen and automatically save it to your device’s Pictures folder.

Now that you know the basics of Print Screen on your Lenovo Windows 7 laptop, you can easily take screenshots of any items on your screen. This feature can come in handy for troubleshooting, documenting, or just sharing information.
