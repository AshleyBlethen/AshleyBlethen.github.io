In this ultimate guide, we will explain step-by-step how to customize your Steam screenshots in 2022.

The first step is to click the “View Screenshots” button in your Steam Library. This will open up the Screenshot Manager window, where you can view, delete, and upload new screenshots. The next step is to set up the in-game screenshots. You can capture the moment you want to save and use it as your screenshot. Steam will automatically detect the game and add it to your screenshots library. You can select the game and adjust the resolution of your screenshot, as well as the time window for the screenshot. This is a useful feature as you can easily create a library of unique screenshots.

Finally, you can stream your gameplay with Steam’s broadcasting feature. You can select to broadcast your gameplay live or to just capture it for later. This will allow you to capture and share your gaming experiences with other Steam users. You can also save your broadcasts and upload them to YouTube or Twitch.

In conclusion, customizing your Steam screenshots in 2022 is an easy and effective way to personalize your gaming experience. With the help of the Screenshot Manager, you can easily customize backgrounds and text for your screenshots, set up in-game captures, and even stream your gaming sessions.
