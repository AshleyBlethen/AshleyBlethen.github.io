3. Add Apps to Your Android Auto Dashboard#

Android Auto is the best way to use your favourite apps in your car. Now, Google has made it even easier with the latest update to Android Auto 2022. With just a few steps, you can quickly add apps to your Android Auto dashboard. Here’s how.

First, open the Android Auto app on your phone and tap the hamburger menu in the top left corner. Then, select Settings and tap Apps. Here, you should see a list of apps that are compatible with Android Auto. Select the apps you wish to add and they’ll appear in your Android Auto dashboard.

Next, connect your phone to the car with either a USB cable or Bluetooth. This will sync the apps you selected to your Android Auto dashboard. You can now launch these apps by tapping the hamburger menu in the bottom right corner of your car’s display.

Finally, if you ever want to edit or remove these apps from your Android Auto dashboard, simply go to the Settings and Apps menus again. Here, you’ll be able to delete or disable any apps you previously added. You can even rearrange the apps’ order and customize their layout.
