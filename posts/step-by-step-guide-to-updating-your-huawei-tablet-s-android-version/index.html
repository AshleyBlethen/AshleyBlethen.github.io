2. Preparation Before Updating#

Updating your Huawei tablet’s version of Android is a great way to improve its performance, get the latest features, and stay secure from the latest security threats. Before you can update your tablet, however, it’s important to properly prepare your device. Here are the steps you should take before updating your Huawei tablet’s Android version.

First, backup any important data that is stored on the device, as updating can cause it to be lost. Make sure you take the time to copy any files, photos, music, and other information you don’t want to lose, either to the cloud, or to an external hard drive.

Next, make sure that the battery is fully charged before beginning the update process. An improperly charged battery can cause problems during the installation process, so plug in your tablet and give it a good few hours to charge up.

Finally, make sure that your tablet has enough storage space to accommodate the new version of Android. Check the device’s memory to see if there is enough space for the new installation, and delete any files or photos that have been stored for a while if needed.

By following these steps, your tablet will be ready to receive the latest version of Android, and you can be sure that your device is prepared and ready.
