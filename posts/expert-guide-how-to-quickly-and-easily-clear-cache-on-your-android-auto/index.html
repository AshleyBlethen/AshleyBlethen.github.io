1. Method to Clear Cache on Android Auto#

Clearing your Android Auto cache can be an important step in solving a number of issues. It is quick and easy to do, allowing you to keep your device running optimally. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to clear your Android Auto cache:

First, open the Android Auto app on your smartphone. You will then be presented with a few options. Select ‘Settings’ and go to ‘Application Manager’.

Next, select the ‘All’ tab to view all of the applications installed on your Android Auto device. Scroll down until you find the Android Auto app, then tap it to open the application details.

Once the application details have been opened, tap the ‘Clear Cache’ button. This will delete all the cached data stored by the application, including any temporary files and files that may have been created by the app.

Finally, restart your Android Auto device and you should now have a cache-free device.
