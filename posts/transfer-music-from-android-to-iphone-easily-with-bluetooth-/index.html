1. Overview of the Bluetooth technology#

Bluetooth is a wireless technology standard used for exchanging data between fixed and mobile devices over short distances using short-wavelength radio transmissions. It was the first technology of its kind when it was introduced in the late 1990s and has since been adopted to connect various devices, from phones to car stereos. With Bluetooth technology, you can transfer music from your Android device to your iPhone easily. All you need is a compatible device and a few simple steps.
