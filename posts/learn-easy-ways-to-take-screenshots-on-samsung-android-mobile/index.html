The screenshot can be customized and edited on Samsung phones. Tap the screenshot notification that appears in the drop-down notification bar. This will open a pop-up window with editing tools, and you can also add doodles, text, and crop the image.

You can also share the screenshot directly via your favorite messaging application or social media app. Long press on the screenshot in the Gallery app and select Share. Choose from one of the applications which appears on the screen.

Finally, you can view your screenshots in the Gallery app. Just open the app and scroll through the folders until you find the Screenshots folder. All your screenshots will be available here.
