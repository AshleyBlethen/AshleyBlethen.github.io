The first step is to backup your data from your Android phone. This can be done by going to Menu > Settings > Chats > Chat Backup > Back up. Once your backup is complete, you need to move the file to your new phone. This can be done by using Google Drive or Dropbox.

The third and final step is to restore the data from the backup file. To do this, you need to go to Menu > Settings > Chats > Chat Backup > Restore. Your WhatsApp data should now be restored and ready to use!

By following these 3 easy steps, you can easily transfer your WhatsApp data from Android to iPhone after setup. No need to worry about data loss or any technicalities; these steps make it fast, easy and hassle-free.
