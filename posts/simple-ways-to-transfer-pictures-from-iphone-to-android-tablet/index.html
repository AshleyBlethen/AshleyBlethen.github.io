2. Transferring Pictures via WiFi#

Transferring pictures from an iPhone to an Android tablet can be done quite easily using either Bluetooth or Wi-Fi. To get started, make sure that Bluetooth is enabled on both devices and then open Settings on the iPhone. Select Bluetooth and then turn it on. Now, go back to the main settings menu and tap General. Here, select Share My Location and then tap Share Indefinitely. This will allow the Bluetooth connection between the iPhone and Android tablet.

Next, open the Files folder on the Android tablet and select the Bluetooth folder. Here, the iPhone should appear in the list of Bluetooth devices. Select the iPhone and enter the Bluetooth pairing code when prompted. Once the two devices are paired, select the pictures you wish to transfer from the iPhone and then select the Android tablet from the list of devices sent to.

Alternatively, you can transfer pictures from the iPhone to the Android tablet using Wi-Fi as well. To do this, first enable Wi-Fi on both devices and then connect them to the same network. Go to the Photos app on the iPhone and select the pictures you wish to transfer. Now, select Share and then choose the Android tablet. Finally, wait for the pictures to be transferred.

With these steps, you can easily transfer pictures from an iPhone to an Android tablet using Bluetooth or Wi-Fi. You can transfer multiple pictures simultaneously using both methods, making the process much faster and more convenient.

3. Transferring Pictures with a USB Connection#

Transferring pictures between devices can be a cumbersome process, but luckily, it is fairly easy to transfer photos from an iPhone to an Android tablet over a USB connection. Here are a few simple steps to help you get started:

  1. Connect your iPhone to your tablet with a USB cable. Allow a few moments for your devices to recognize each other.

  2. Unlock your iPhone and on the device, select “Trust this device.” On the tablet, open the “File explorer” application.

  3. On the iPhone, open the “Photos” app. Locate the photo(s) you want to transfer and select “Share” to send them. On the tablet, select the option “Receive files from other devices” and then select “Receive”.

  4. That’s it! The photo(s) will now be transferred to your tablet and can be found in the device’s “Photos” app.

By following these easy steps, you can quickly and easily transfer pictures from an iPhone to an Android tablet over a USB connection.
