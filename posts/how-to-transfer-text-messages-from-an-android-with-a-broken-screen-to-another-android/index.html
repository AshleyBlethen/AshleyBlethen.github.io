First, you’ll need a USB OTG cable. This is a type of adapter that contains two USB connectors - one on either end. Connect one end of the cable to your broken phone and the other to to a laptop or tablet. Then, download and install an application called Android Transfer on your laptop or tablet.

Once Android Transfer is installed, open the app and plug the other end of the USB cable into the laptop or tablet. You should see the full contents of your broken phone, including any text messages you’ve received. Select the messages you want to transfer, and then click the “Transfer” button.

The final step is to connect your new phone to the laptop or tablet. To do this, you’ll need to download and install Android Transfer on the new device as well. Open the app on your new phone and connect it to the laptop or tablet via USB. Then, select the messages you want to transfer and hit the “Transfer” button. The messages will be sent to your new device.
